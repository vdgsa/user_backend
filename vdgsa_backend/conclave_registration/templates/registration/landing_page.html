Before you begin your Conclave registration, make sure that you have familiarized yourself with the options and decided whether you want to be in the Regular Program (either full time or part time) or in one of the special programs, such as Consort Cooperative, Seasoned Players, etc., or one of the free Beginners' Programs. You should also have an idea about which classes you would like to request. Remember that you can find more complete information in the Conclave Brochure.

The contact information from your membership account will be used for Conclave. If you'd like to change it, please do so on the My Account section of the website.

Once you select your program, you'll go to a series of pages that will need to be filled out and saved. Clicking the yellow "Save and Continue" button on the bottom of each page will take you through them sequentially; you can navigate to any page by clicking on the left-hand menu.

If you select a programs other than Regular, please be aware that there may be more requirements listed on the Programs page. Vendors should register for the Regular Curriculum (part-time for 0-1 class, full-time for 2-3 classes) unless they plan to participate in one of the special programs.

If you have any questions while applying, please contact the Conclave Management Team Spork or the Website Committee . If you would like help by telephone, please send a text message, saying "Conclave registration help needed" to Kathy Schenley or Becca Humphrey. Their telephone numbers can be found here.
