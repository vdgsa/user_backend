How do you renew your membership?

Go to hume.vdgsa.org and click 'Renew your membership'. You can also get there through the My Account button on the Members' Area.

Once you've renewed, you'll be back in your account, so you might as well check to make sure that your information is up-to-date. You can edit your contact information if it's changed, update your Teaching or Commercial Member profile (if you have one), or even update your privacy preferences (towards the bottom).

If you still want to renew by mail, there's a slightly different process. On the Login page, instead of choosing one of the buttons, click on "Benefits of Membership." At the bottom of that page is a printable membership form that you can download, print out, and mail to me. Note that our dues changed in 2024: $40 for members in the US, Canada, and all other countries in North, South, and Central America; $45 for members outside these areas. Note that you won't be able to access the Members' Area of the website until I receive and process your check.

One important change with our new website is that your membership becomes inactive from the website's perspective the day after your membership expires. We still consider you an active member, but you can't access the Members' Area on the website until you renew. You don't want to miss any of the other wonderful things accessible only to members on the website, so renew now! Don't forget you can contact me if you have questions or problems at membership@vdgsa.org.
